Hello everyone. This is my first entry for my blog. I hope you all enjoy.

I honestly don't know what to put. Maybe some stuff about the Rec Room and stuff like that. So for Rec Room, it's like an arcade with a bar, typical. The games there are the usual, PAC-MAN, Wheel of Fortune, table hockey, etc. The food is pretty good, the serving time is slow, but this isn't a fast food chain, so I can understand. The ticket prices are fairly ok, $35 (Canadian) for 160 tickets, pretty ok. Idk, it's pretty nice there. A great way to gamble away your savings. <b>8/10</b>
